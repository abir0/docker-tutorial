About Bangladesh

Bangladesh, officially the People's Republic of Bangladesh, is a country in South Asia. It is the eighth-most populous country in the world, with a population exceeding 169 million people in an area of 148,460 square kilometers.

Known as the "Land of Rivers," Bangladesh is crisscrossed by more than 700 rivers, creating a beautiful landscape of waterways and fertile plains. The country boasts incredible natural beauty, from the world's longest natural sea beach to the largest mangrove forest.

Beyond its natural wonders, Bangladesh is known for its warm hospitality, rich cultural heritage, and resilient people. The Bengali language and culture unite the nation, with literature, music, and art playing important roles in daily life.
